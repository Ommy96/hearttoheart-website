Why Volunteer?

Your time and skills can transform lives

Volunteering with Heart to Heart Organization is a rewarding way to make a direct impact in the lives of vulnerable children and families in Kibera and Kawangware.

Our volunteers bring diverse skills and perspectives that enhance our programs and help us reach more people in need. Whether you're passionate about education, nutrition, or community development, we have opportunities for you.

Volunteers gain valuable experience working in community development, build meaningful relationships, and contribute to sustainable change in one of Nairobi's most underserved communities.
