Our Story

Founded in 2010 by local community leaders, Heart to Heart Organization began with a simple mission: to provide essential support to vulnerable children and families in Kibera and Kawangware.

What started as a small feeding program has grown into a comprehensive organization offering education support, vocational training, health services, and community empowerment initiatives.

Today, we serve over 2,000 children and families annually, transforming lives and building hope for a brighter future.
